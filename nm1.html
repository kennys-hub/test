Property Lot Description - Rio del Oro 🏡

Property Description 🌄

This 0.28-acre property in Rio del Oro, Unit 28, Valencia County, New Mexico, is a sustainable off-grid homestead. Measuring 77’ east-west by 156’ north-south, it features a 2-story container home, garage, shed, heat pump, septic system (ATU tank and drain field), cistern, jacuzzi, doghouse, swales, and a gravel path. The property is zoned RR-1 (Rural Residential) and is oriented with the street on the south (y=0), north boundary at y=156, west at x=0, and east at x=77. A 5 kW solar system powers the home, supported by 27 kWh battery storage, making it fully off-grid. 🌞

Home and Utilities 🏠

  • Container Home 🏠: 2-story, 900 sq ft, built from two 40’ × 8’ ground-level containers and one 20’ × 8’ upstairs section. Includes an electric stove (5 kW), heat pump indoor air handler, upgraded insulation (R-30 walls, R-50 roof), 32 sq ft south-facing windows, and thermal mass for passive solar heating. Located at columns 3–6, rows 2–4 (20’–60’, 28.36’–70.9’).
  • Garage 🚗: 20’ × 20’, contains solar batteries (27 kWh), inverter, water pump, vehicle parking, and tools. Located at columns 2–3, rows 2–3 (15’–30’, 28.36’–42.54’).
  • Shed 🛠️: 6’ × 6’, stores garden tools, cistern accessories, and miscellaneous items. Located at column 2, row 5 (15’, 65’).
  • Heat Pump ❄️: 2-ton (24,000 BTU, 2.4 kW), provides cooling and primary heating. Located at column 2, row 4 (15’, 56.72’).
  • ATU Tank 🚽: BioMicrobics MicroFAST 0.5 tank (8’ × 4’), part of the septic system. Located at column 6, row 7 (60’, 100’–104’).
  • Drain Field 💧: 10’ × 10’, disperses treated effluent. Located at column 6, row 6 (57’, 80’–90’).
  • Cistern 💦: 2,000-gallon rainwater cistern (10’ × 7.5’), collects runoff. Located at column 6, row 2 (60’, 25’).
  • Jacuzzi 🛁: 7’ × 7’, for relaxation. Located at column 4, row 5 (35’, 65’).
  • Doghouse 🐶: 3’ × 3’, pet shelter. Located at column 2, row 9 (15’, 127.62’).
  • Swales 🌊: Runoff mitigations (50’ × 5’), manage water flow. Located at columns 2–6, row 10 (15’–60’, 141.8’–156’).
  • Path 🚶: Gravel path to the jacuzzi (3’ × 10’), connects the home to the jacuzzi. Located at columns 3–6, row 5 (20’–60’, 65’).
  • Solar System ☀️: 5 kW, roof-mounted on the home, produces ~20 kWh/day.

Applicable Laws 📜

Valencia County RR-1 Zoning (Article 5, Section 154.071)

  • Front Setback: 25’ from the street (y=0).
  • Side Setback: 10’ from side property lines (x=0, x=77).
  • Rear Setback: 10’ from the rear property line (y=156).
  • Lot Coverage: Maximum 35% for all structures.

NMAC § 20.7.3 (Liquid Waste Treatment and Disposal)

  • ATU Tank and Drain Field Setbacks:
  • 5’ from property lines.
  • 10’ from buildings.
  • 4’ from non-paved surfaces (e.g., paths).
  • 50’ from water storage systems (e.g., cistern).
