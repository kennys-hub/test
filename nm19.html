Kenny's Sustainable, Off-Grid Container Home in Valencia County, NM 🌄

Property Description 🌵

🌟 Breakthrough: Advanced septic system ensures zero pollution risk to the Rio Grande! 🌟

Tucked into the high desert of Canyon del Rio, this 0.28-acre lot is located at 34.6911, -106.5575 in Valencia County, NM. Located in Rio del Oro, Unit 28, Lot 25, Block 27, the lot features a 3.33% slope and sandy loam soil ideal for sustainable building.

  • 🌊 Arroyo enters at (0,75) at 35° and exits at (80,35) at 30°
  • 📏 Topography: 10 ft elevation drop, directs runoff toward the arroyo
  • 🛡️ Zoned RR-1, FEMA Zone X – minimal flood risk

Access and Proximity 🛤️

1–2 miles dirt road → 5 miles gravel → 11 miles paved to Belen (total ~18 miles, 25–30 mins). Albuquerque: 35 miles via Rail Runner or I-25.

Setbacks & Buildable Area 📐

  • Front (SW): 30 ft
  • Rear (NE): 15 ft
  • Side (SE): 15 ft
  • Arroyo (NW): 35 ft (100 ft for septic)

Buildable: 30 ft × 105 ft = 3,150 sq ft (26.25%) ✅ RR-1 compliant

Container Home Design 🏠

  • Main Home: 900 sq ft (three containers), solar powered, R-13 insulation
  • Garage: 18' × 10', Shed: 6' × 4', Jacuzzi: 6' × 6', Doghouse: 3' × 3'

Utilities & Systems 🔋

  • ☀️ 5kW Solar Array – powers 2-ton heat pump
  • 🚽 BioMicrobics MicroFAST septic system + sensors
  • 💧 Elevated mound field + gravel filter + 5,000 gal cistern
  • 🌵 Cacti rows, swales, retention basins for runoff and filtering
