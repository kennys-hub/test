Property Description 🌄

Nestled in the stunning Canyon del Rio, Valencia County, New Mexico, this 0.28-acre lot (measuring 80' x 150' or 12,000 sq ft) offers the perfect opportunity for sustainable living. The property is designed for off-grid living with all necessary utilities and is located in an ideal rural setting for peace, privacy, and a connection to nature. This land is zoned RR-1 and is perfectly suited for Kenny- looking for a blend of comfort, energy independence, and natural beauty. 🏜️

The Home and Utilities 🏠

The centerpiece of this property is a solar-powered, two-story, 900 sq ft home, built with adobe and stucco materials. Designed with eco-conscious principles, the home minimizes energy use while maximizing comfort.

  • 🏠Two-Story Home: The 900 sq ft home offers a sustainable, off-grid living experience. Features include thermal mass construction, R-30/R-50 insulation, and 32 sq ft of windows. The home is powered by solar energy and includes an electric stove, heat pump, and ample natural light.
  • 🚗Garage: A 20' × 20' garage with ample space for solar batteries, an inverter, a water pump, tools, and parking.
  • 🛠️Shed: A 6’ × 6’ storage shed for gardening tools and cistern equipment.
  • ❄️Heat Pump: A 2-ton system (2.4 kW) ensures efficient climate control.
  • 🚽ATU Tank: A BioMicrobics MicroFAST 0.5 tank (8’ × 4’) for waste management.
  • 💧Drain Field: A 10’ × 10’ drain field for effluent dispersal.
  • 💦Cistern: The property includes a 2,000-gallon cistern for water storage.
  • 🛁Jacuzzi: A 7’ × 7’ jacuzzi for relaxation.
  • 🐶Doghouse: A 3’ × 3’ doghouse for pets.
  • 🌊Swales: Designed for water management, swales measure 50' × 5'.
  • 🚶Path: A 3’ × 10’ gravel path connecting the home to the jacuzzi.
  • ☀️Solar Power: A 5 kW solar array on the roof produces around 20 kWh of energy per day.
