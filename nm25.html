Canyon del Rio Overview 🌵

Kenny's Sustainable, Off-Grid Ecotech Container Home in Valencia County, NM

Property Description 🏜️

Breakthrough: Advanced septic system and eco-friendly design ensure zero pollution risk to the Rio Grande, with low WOTUS likelihood for the arroyo! 🌿

Tucked into the high desert of Canyon del Rio, Valencia County, NM, this 0.28-acre lot (80' × 150', 12,000 sq ft) at coordinates 34.6911, -106.5575 is Kenny’s vision for sustainable off-grid living. Located in Rio del Oro, Unit 28, Lot 25, Block 27, the lot is a rectangle oriented due north, with the longer axis (150 ft) running north-south (y-axis) and the shorter axis (80 ft) running east-west (x-axis). It has a 3.33% slope from NW (5400 ft) to SE (5390 ft). The Valencia County plat has been corrected to reflect the lot’s true position. 🌄

Key Features 🌱
  • 🌾Soil: Well-draining sandy loam, ideal for construction and supporting a small garden with water-efficient irrigation.
  • 🏞️Arroyo: Camino Rio, 20–30 ft southeast of the lot’s true southern boundary, is ephemeral, dry 99.9% of the time. It runs diagonally southwest to northeast, connecting to the Rio Grande during extreme events.
  • 🏠Topography: 10 ft elevation drop (3.33% NW-SE), directing runoff toward the SE corner and into the arroyo below.
  • 📏Zoning & Safety: Zoned RR-1, FEMA Zone X (minimal flood risk). Eco-friendly runoff management includes swales, basins, and a 5,000-gallon cistern.
Access and Proximity 🚗

This off-grid ecotech homestead blends seclusion with accessibility. From the lot, it’s a short drive to Rio del Oro Loop South, followed by 5 miles on gravel to the Manzano Expressway, and then 11 paved miles to Belen. Albuquerque is about 50 minutes away via I-25 N. 🛣️

Setbacks and Compliance 📜

The lot’s longer axis (150 ft) runs north-south (y-axis), and the shorter axis (80 ft) runs east-west (x-axis). The Camino Rio arroyo, 20–30 ft southeast of the lot’s southern boundary, poses minimal zoning challenges.
